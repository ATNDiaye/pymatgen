class RelaxedInterstitial(struct_list, energy_list, valence_dict)[source]¶

Stores the relaxed supercell structures for each interstitial Used to compute formation energies, displacement of atoms near the the interstitial

Args:
struct_list:
List of structures(supercells). The first structure should represent relaxed bulk structure and the subsequent ones interstitial structures (with the extra interstitial site appended at the end).
energy_list:
List of energies for relaxed interstitial structures. The first energy should correspond to bulk structure
valence_dict:
Valences of elements in dictionary form
